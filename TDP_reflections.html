Reflective Essay on The Data Professional Module

Introduction

In the midst of the 4th industrial revolution, the discipline of Data Science has emerged as a cornerstone for leveraging big data, transforming it into actionable insights. This module has been instrumental in highlighting the significance of Data Science within this era, emphasizing not just the technical skills required but also the ethical, legal, and social responsibilities of data professionals.
